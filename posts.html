Movie Review: "Those Who Wish Me Dead" (2.8/5 Stars) (5/18/2021)

So yesterday I decided to go against my better judgement and took a trip to the theater to see Angelina Jolie's new flick "Those Who Wish Me Dead" directed by Taylor Sheridan. Sheridan is widley recognized for films such as "Hell or High Water" and "Sicario". I am a fan of both films (the former moreso than the latter). If I were to rank the three, however, I would certainly rank Sheridan's new film the worst of the three.

In the film, Littlefinger (from GOT; the dad in this flick), is killed off early on and his situation is never really explained. Apparently, he discovered some big accounting discrepancies and screwwed with the wrong guys. Those same very guys begin to hunt LittleFinger (yes still referencing GOT) and his kid in order to kill them and cover there tails. In the end, Jolie and the Kid get away from the men that were orginally following them. They live happily ever after. THE END.

But why were they being followed in the first place?? This is a huge question that the writers never answered leaving the audience (ME) utterly dissatisfied. The movie seems to be all over the place at times and a better job could have been done to keep the audience up to speed with what is going on. Besides that this is an ok film. At times, it got stale and in my opinion is not a must see by any means. I give it 2.8/5 stars and would recommend you wait til it comes to TV, whenever that may be...
